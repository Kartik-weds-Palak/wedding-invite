How We Met

On Effervescence's final night, amid rhythm and reckless youth, our eyes collided—a lightning strike disguised as chance. She spoke first, her voice a door I'd been searching for. Coffee became our ritual, lectures abandoned like old skins. She showed me Allahabad's hidden bones; I learned love speaks in different tones. Bangalore called me away after two months, yet distance only sharpened what we'd found—something wild, untameable. Years later, we stand at forever's edge, no longer strangers dancing, but home.

- Kartik & Palak

Wedding Events

Join us in celebrating our special days

22
November

Mehendi

5:00 PM onwards

It's one of the oldest traditional Indian pre-wedding ceremony where intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet. This will be a celebratory festival with vibrant colors, music, dancing, and lots of joy!

Dress Code: Ethnic Indian Wear

Ladies can wear bright & vivid outfits, and gentlemen can sport vibrant kurtas with pants or pyjamas.

22
November

Engagement

8:00 PM onwards

Join us for an elegant evening as we formally announce our commitment to each other. An evening filled with love, laughter, music, and celebration!

Dress Code: Western Attire

A semi-formal contemporary look. Ladies and gentlemen can dress in fashionable Western outfits.

23
November

Haldi

11:00 AM onwards

A traditional ceremony involving the application of turmeric paste on the bride and groom. This auspicious ritual is believed to bless the couple and give them a natural glow before the wedding.

Dress Code: Comfortable Indian Wear

Wear something you don't mind getting turmeric on! Bright yellows and traditional attire recommended.

23
November

Wedding Ceremony

8:00 PM onwards

This is the moment we've been waiting for! Join us as we take our vows and begin our journey as husband and wife. The ceremony will be followed by dinner and celebration into the night.

Dress Code: Indo-Western

Ladies can flaunt sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, or elegant dresses. Gentlemen can wear suits, blazers, or band gala waistcoats with shirts.
